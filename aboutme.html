About Me!

Passionate Writer

Hey There! I'm Sunny, a 21 year old aspiring writer from Argentina who daydreams too much. Other than the short stories I'll be sharing with you, I'm also working on a fantasy novel, which i hope gets to see the light of day someday. Some of my all-time favorite books are Alice's Adventures in Wonderland by Lewis Carroll, One by Sarah Crossan, A Christmas Carol by Charles Dickens, and the Percy Jackson series by Rick Riordan. Besides writing and reading, I am into videogames, animation, musicals, superhero films, among other things. From the bottom of my heart, thank you for giving my writing a chance.
